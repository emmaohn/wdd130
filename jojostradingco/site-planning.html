Overview

Purpose

I want to send a message people with a passion for fashion, but may not have the means to support the growth of a conventional wardrobe. I want to contribute to a change in the view that fashion has to be new or luxury. Second hand fashion is a great way to express yourself, give life to an old piece of clothing, and subsequently reduce the environmental impact of discarded clothing. Did you know that the amount of textile waste created annually is an estimated 92 million tons? By inspiring fashion-oriented individuals to pursue this kind of reuse, the emotional and environmental effects abound. I would love to create a blog where there is a community that is passionate about second hand fashion, and a community that cares about the world we live in, even if it is a residual idea from becoming more involved in the second hand community.

Audience

I want to market towards college age, teens to young adults. I want to reach people who are just beginning to understand and know who they really are, and are can share in the feeling of becoming an adult. I want to connect with a population of people who care.
